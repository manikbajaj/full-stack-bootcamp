Combinator Selectors in CSS

Descendant Selector (space)

This paragraph is a descendant of the div.

This nested paragraph is also a descendant of the outer div.

Child Selector (>)

This paragraph is a direct child of the div and will be bold.

This nested paragraph is not a direct child.

Adjacent Sibling Selector (+)

This paragraph is an adjacent sibling of the previous h2.

This paragraph is not an adjacent sibling of the previous h2.

General Sibling Selector (~)

This paragraph is a general sibling of the h2.

This paragraph is also a general sibling of the h2.
