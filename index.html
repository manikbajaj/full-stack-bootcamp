CSS Overflow Property Demonstration

Overflow: Visible

This content overflows, and it is visible outside the box.

Content outside the box will be shown.

Instructions: By default, overflow is visible, meaning content that doesn't fit within the container will overflow and be displayed outside the boundaries of the element.

Overflow: Hidden

This content overflows but is clipped, and you cannot see it.

Hidden content will not be visible.

Instructions: With overflow: hidden, any content that exceeds the container's size is clipped, and the overflowing content is not displayed.

Overflow: Scroll

This content overflows and has scrollbars. Scroll to see more content.

Scrollbars will always be visible.

Instructions: With overflow: scroll, the container will always show scrollbars, whether or not the content overflows.

Overflow: Auto

This content overflows, but scrollbars will only appear when needed.

Scrollbars appear automatically.

Instructions: With overflow: auto, scrollbars will appear only if the content exceeds the size of the container, otherwise they remain hidden.
